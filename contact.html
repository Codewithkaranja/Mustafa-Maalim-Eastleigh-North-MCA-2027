Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to get a quick response from the campaign?

For the fastest response, we recommend using WhatsApp or calling our campaign hotline at +254 723 460 538 our team monitors these channels closely and typically responds within an hour during business hours.

Can I schedule a meeting with Hon. Mustafa Maalim?

Yes! We encourage constituents to meet with Hon. mustafa maalim. Please contact our office to schedule an appointment. We also hold regular town hall meetings and community forums where you can interact directly.

How can I report an issue in my neighborhood?

You can report community issues through any of our contact channels. For faster resolution, include specific details about the location and nature of the issue. We also have ward representatives who can assist with local concerns.

Do you have volunteers who can help with community events?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated team of volunteers ready to support community initiatives. Contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@mustafamaalim.com to discuss your event needs.

How can I get campaign materials for my business or organization?

We appreciate community support! Please visit any of our campaign offices to pick up materials, or contact us to arrange delivery. You can also download digital materials from our Media page.
