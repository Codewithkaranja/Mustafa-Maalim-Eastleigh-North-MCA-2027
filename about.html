About Hon. Maalim

Visionary⭐ Community Champion ⭐ Brown for Eastleigh North

Biography

Hon. Mustafa Maalim is a progressive leader, seasoned community organizer, and dedicated public servant aspiring to serve the people of Eastleigh North Ward as their Member of County Assembly. Born and raised in Eastleigh North, he is deeply grounded in the lived realities of the Ward and carries a lifelong commitment to advancing the social and economic wellbeing of its people.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in community development, youth empowerment, and public service, mustafa has built a reputation as a practical problem-solver and a unifying leader. He has worked closely with grassroots groups, faith-based organizations, youth and women initiatives, and development partners to champion inclusive growth, job creation, and improved access to opportunities for all.

Mustafa’s leadership journey from grassroots activism to positions of responsibility has shaped his strong belief in people-centered governance, transparency, and accountability. He understands that effective representation requires listening to the people, advocating for their priorities, and delivering measurable results.

As an aspiring Member of County Assembly, Hon. Mustafa Maalim is driven by a bold vision to provide transformative leadership, strengthen service delivery, promote youth and economic empowerment, and ensure Eastleigh North Ward has a strong, principled, and effective voice in Parliament. His campaign is anchored on unity, integrity, and sustainable development putting the people of Eastleigh North first.

Explore My Journey

Leadership Journey

2016 - 2018

Community Organizing Beginnings

Started as a youth leader in Eastleigh North, organizing community clean-ups and youth sports programs. Founded the Eastleigh North Youth Empowerment Initiative that reached over 500 young people.

2016 - 2020

Education & Professional Development

Graduated with honors in Political Science and Public Administration from the University of Nairobi. Simultaneously served as Chairman of the Eastleigh North Community Development Association.

2021 to Date

Grassroots Advocacy

Led successful campaigns for improved public services in Eastleigh North, including better waste management and upgraded healthcare facilities. Coordinated relief efforts during flooding emergencies.

2022 To Date

Youth Empowerment Expansion

Established the Eastleigh North Skills Development Center, providing vocational training to over 1,200 youth. Launched mentorship programs connecting young people with industry professionals.

2021 - 2025

Policy & Governance

Served as Special Advisor on Youth Affairs to the County Government, influencing policies that benefited over 50,000 young people across the county. Championed digital literacy programs.

2022 - Present

Eastleigh North Movement

Launched the Eastleigh North 2027 campaign with a comprehensive vision for Eastleigh North's development. Building a people-centered movement focused on transformative leadership and community-driven progress.

Creative solutions for modern challenges through technology, research, and forward-thinking approaches to community development.
