Vision & Agenda for Eastleigh North 2027

A comprehensive development blueprint for transformative growth and community prosperity

The Eastleigh North Vision

My vision for Eastleigh North is a Ward where every resident has access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities; where our youth are empowered to become innovators and job creators; where our environment is protected and enhanced; and where community participation drives development decisions.

By 2027, we will transform Eastleigh North into a model Ward that demonstrates how visionary leadership, community engagement, and innovative solutions can create lasting positive change for all residents.

Explore Our Development Pillars

Development Pillars

Youth Empowerment & Economic Opportunities

Our youth are not just the future—they are the present. We will create an ecosystem that nurtures talent, fosters innovation, and provides tangible economic opportunities for young people in Eastleigh North.

Eastleigh North Youth Skills Academy

Established a state-of-the-art vocational training center offering courses in technology, entrepreneurship, and trades with direct industry connections.

Youth Innovation Fund

Create a KSh 50 million revolving fund to provide seed capital, grants, and low-interest loans for youth-led startups and small businesses.

Digital Talent Pipeline

Partner with tech companies to train 1,000 youth annually in digital skills with guaranteed internship opportunities and job placements.

Quality Education for All

Education is the great equalizer. We will ensure every child in Eastleigh North has access to quality education from early childhood through tertiary levels, with special focus on digital literacy and 21st-century skills.

Digital Classrooms Initiative

Equip all public schools in Eastleigh North with computer labs, internet connectivity, and digital learning resources by 2026.

Comprehensive Scholarship Program

Established a KSh 30 million annual scholarship fund for bright but needy students from primary school through university.

School Infrastructure Upgrade

Rehabilitate and modernize 15 primary schools and 5 secondary schools with better facilities, libraries, and science laboratories.

Accessible Healthcare Services

Health is wealth. We will ensure every resident has access to affordable, quality healthcare through upgraded facilities, community health programs, and preventive care initiatives.

Community Health Center Expansion

Build 3 new community health centers and upgrade 5 existing facilities with modern equipment and specialized services.

Mobile Health Clinics

Launch 2 fully equipped mobile clinics to serve remote areas of Eastleigh North with regular medical camps and immunization drives.

Specialist Outreach Program

Partner with major hospitals to bring specialist medical services to Eastleigh North residents at subsidized rates.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation (2027-2028)

Community consultation, planning, and initiation of high-impact quick-win projects.

Progress 85%

Phase 2: Implementation (2028-2030)

Full-scale implementation of major projects and programs across all pillars.

Progress 45%

Phase 3: Scaling (2030-2031)

Expansion of successful initiatives and introduction of advanced programs.

Progress 20%

Phase 4: Sustainability (2031-2032)

Ensuring long-term impact through community ownership and institutionalization.

Progress 5%
