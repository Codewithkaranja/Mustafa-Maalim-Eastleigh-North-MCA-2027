About Hon. Mustafa Maalim

Biography

Hon. Mustafa Maalim is a progressive leader, seasoned community organizer, and dedicated public servant aspiring to serve the people of Eastleigh North Ward as their Member of County Assembly. Born and raised in Eastleigh North, he is deeply grounded in the lived realities of the Ward and carries a lifelong commitment to advancing the social and economic wellbeing of its people.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in community development, youth empowerment, and public service, Mustafa Maalim has built a reputation as a practical problem-solver and a unifying leader.
